Alessandro Manenti

Lugano, Switzerland

Hi! I'm Alessandro, I am a Ph.D. student in Graph Deep Learning.
I'm part of the Graph Machine Learning Group which is a research team of the Swiss AI Lab IDSIA, under the supervision of Professor Cesare Alippi.

I develop methods that learn latent relational structures to empower deep learning models — and I love exploring any model that uses latent-space representations, with a particular focus on discrete latent spaces.

Publications

Learning Latent Graph Structures and their Uncertainty
ICML 2025

In this paper, we highlight a challenge in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), demonstrating that traditional methods struggle to learn hidden relational structures and their uncertainties. We propose a new approach that jointly learns these latent graph structures while enhancing prediction accuracy, and we provide empirical evidence to support the developed theoretical results.
