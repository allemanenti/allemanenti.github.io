Publications

Learning Latent Graph Structures and their Uncertainty

In this paper, we highlight a challenge in Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), demonstrating that traditional methods struggle to learn hidden relational structures and their uncertainties. We propose a new approach that jointly learns these latent graph structures while enhancing prediction accuracy, and we provide empirical evidence to support the developed theoretical results.
